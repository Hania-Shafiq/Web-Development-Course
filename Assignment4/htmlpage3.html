Grilled Cheese Sandwich

        

        
        

       
 Grilled Cheese Sandwich

        
Ingredients:

        
        2 slices of bread (any kind you prefer)
        2 slices of cheddar cheese (or any cheese you like)
        2 tablespoons butter

        
Instructions:

        Preheat Pan: Heat a skillet over medium heat.
        Butter the Bread: Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
        Assemble Sandwich: Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the skillet. Add the cheese slices on top of the bread, then place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side up.
        Grill Sandwich: Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 2-3 minutes per side.
        Serve: Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately, preferably with a side of tomato soup.
